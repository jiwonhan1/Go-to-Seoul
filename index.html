Welcome To Korea

Soul-Seoul

Korea is an exciting country that has vivid and soulful spirits everywhere.
Seoul is a capital city of Korea where has undergone a very fast development for past 50 years. Each area has a their own story which you can time-travel between past and present.

The blazing neon, the blaring K-pop, the beckoning street food, the beautiful natural escapes and the shopping. There are many reasons why Seoul is a full of souls city.

Gyeongbokgung Palace

The main royal palace of the Joseon dynasty.

Address: 161 Sajik-ro, Sejongno, Jongno-gu, Seoul, South Korea

Deoksugung

A walled compound of palaces in Seoul that was inhabited by members of Korea's royal family during the Joseon monarchy.

Address: 199 Sejong-daero, Jeong-dong, Jung-gu, Seoul, South Korea

Gwanghwamun Gate

The main and largest gate of Gyeongbokgung Palace, in Jongno-gu, Seoul, South Korea.

Address: 161 Sajik-ro, Sejongno, Jongno-gu, Seoul, South Korea

Arts & Culturebrush
N Seoul Tower

A communication and observation tower located on Namsan Mountain in central Seoul, South Korea.

Address: 161 Sajik-ro, Sejongno, Jongno-gu, Seoul, South Korea

Bukchon Hanok Village

A Korean traditional village in Seoul with a long history located on the top of a hill between Gyeongbok Palace, Changdeok Palace and Jongmyo Royal Shrine.

Address: 37 Gahoe-dong, Jongno-gu, Seoul, South Korea

Myeong-dong

A commercial area, being one of Seoul's main shopping, parade route and tourism districts.

Address: Myeong-dongJung-gu, Seoul, South Korea
